We live quality

Our quality standard

To offer you the best quality, we're always busy and testing the products in one of our awesome testing laboratories in each city we're currently available. We have created a valuation system with decimals up to 10 to let you know, which mark we gave the product. The best quality valuation our products can get is a decimal between 8 and 10. A valuation between 6 and 8 decimals means still a good quality. Products with a valuation from 4 to 6 have a normal quality, but it could be, that you won't be happy with it as long as with higher valued products. Products which got a 1 to 4 we won't offer you, but still you will know about all results, when you watch our weekly quality control results, which we will send you when you have signed up.
